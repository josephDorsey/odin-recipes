Chicken Wrap

  • Chicken Patty or Shredded Chicken
  • Spinach
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Cucumber
  • Your choice of condiment
    1. Steam or warm up your tortilla, assuming your meat is already warmed up
    2. After steaming your tortilla place it down and add your condiment choice to it
    3. Next add the vegetables
    4. Then add your chicken at the end
    5. Wrap your tortilla up and boom!
